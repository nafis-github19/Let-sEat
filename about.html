Group Project for CUNY TechWorks,Queensborough Community College (QCC) CUNY.

Let's Eat connects people with great local resturant.

Overview

Let's Eat is the restaurant discovery platform to turn to for trustworthy, efficient, and fun-to-read guides and reviews in from your neighborhood. A mix of user-generated content and our own expertise, all the information on the website serves one purpose: to help you find the perfect restaurant .This is a basic project.Improvement will be on going .
